This release is about accuracy and honesty.

Clearer connection details: your network name and signal now show wherever your platform allows, with a plain, actionable reason when they don't (for example, "Needs Location permission" on macOS) instead of a vague "Unavailable."

New: a short explainer of how the Toolbox measures throughput, and why that number can differ from a speed test.

Plus consistency and reference fixes across the tools and refreshed tool pricing.

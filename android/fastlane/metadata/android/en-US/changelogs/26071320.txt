FIXED: Test My Connection could show a Wi-Fi signal, a Wi-Fi data rate, and Wi-Fi advice while you were on cellular with no Wi-Fi at all. The number was a stale cached reading from an earlier Wi-Fi network, labeled Live. It also fed the Wi-Fi vs internet verdict, so the app told a phone with no Wi-Fi to boost its Wi-Fi. The bug was present in earlier releases. The app now says plainly when you are off Wi-Fi, and shows no reading, no rate, no advice.

This release adds a Field and Trade Reference library for Wi-Fi professionals working on real job sites.

New reference sections cover the built environment you rarely find in one place: enclosure ratings (IP and NEMA), hazardous-location rules, the electrical-code details that affect a wireless install, safety and site-access basics, and the credentials a project needs.

New interactive decoders read vendor status LEDs and model numbers, with honest markers wherever a meaning is not publicly documented.

Every reference sheet now downloads as a PDF to save or share from the field.

Also new: a short, plain explainer of the difference between Wi-Fi, cellular, and internet, and why a strong signal is not the same as a working connection.

New in 1.8.0

Roaming Log, upgraded. Each roam now shows the band, channel, and signal for both the access point you left and the one you joined, so you can see whether a roam was an upgrade. The BSSID now shows the identifying tail, and Copy and Share are back and improved.

New references. Packet Decode (IPv4, IPv6, TCP, UDP, and ICMP header anatomy, TCP flags and connection states, and ICMP codes), Find the Switch and Port (LLDP and CDP), TCP/IP filters in the Wireshark reference, and nmap, iperf3, tcpdump, and tshark in the command reference.

Fixes. Test My Connection no longer shows a stale network name when the live network name is unavailable, plus stability and polish.
